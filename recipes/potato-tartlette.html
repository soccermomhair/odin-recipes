Potato Tartlette

Description

Tartlette, tartiflette, you get the idea

This is essentially, sliced potatoes au gratin. Throw some other fun stuff in there as desired

Ingredients

  • sliced potatoes (yukon preferably)
  • butter or margarine
  • cheese (sliced or grated)
  • optional: breadcrumbs, onions

Steps

  1. Grease sides of pan with butter
  2. Slice potatoes about 1/4in thick
  3. Arrange slices evenly over bottom layer of pan
  4. Sprinkle cheese over
  5. Repeat these last two steps while interspersing optional ingredients and spices
  6. Bake at 400 degrees fahrenheit covered with parchment and foil until potato slices are easily pierced with a fork
