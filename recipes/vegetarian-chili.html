Vegetarian Chili

Description

There are infinite ways to make vegetarian chili

The idea that you'd need meat in here is nonsense. But how your body handles bean is another thing.

Ingredients

  • beans of whichever sort(s)
  • chopped onion
  • minced garlic
  • pepper of your preference
  • other vegetables of your choosing
  • vegetable or olive oil

Steps

  1. Let beans soak overnight or a minimum of eight hours
  2. Simmer garlic and onion in oil at the bottom of a large pot until translucent
  3. Add peppers and/or other vegetables best sauteed for another couple minutes
  4. Add six to eight cups of water proportional to bean amount
  5. Season to taste
  6. Simmer for at least an hour until beans are tender
