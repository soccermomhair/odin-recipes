Salad Deluxe

Description

It's like a salad but fancier

Ingredients

  • chopped lettuce
  • chopped red onion
  • cheese (sliced or grated)
  • cooked beans
  • hearts of palm

Steps

  1. Chop up all ingredients - you don't want that awkward fork grab
  2. Mix all ingredients, be sure to have a big enough bowl to do so
  3. Add any fun toppings like those spices you can get at Trader Joe's or some fun textured thing
  4. Drizzle with salad dressing or oil and vinegar
